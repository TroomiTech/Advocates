Affiliates: The Broad Reach

The Affiliate Program leverages established marketing platforms to extend Troomi's reach across the web. It's a performance-driven model managed by industry experts for maximum impact and efficiency.

Model: Pay-Per-Action (PPA). Compensation is based on a successful purchase made through an affiliate link.

Management: This program is professionally managed by Advertise Purple, a top-tier affiliate marketing agency.

Platform: Operations are run through ShareASale (integrating with Awin), a vast and reputable affiliate network.

A partnership between Troomi, management agency, and affiliate network.

Commission per sale varies by influencer tier.

Influencers: The Content Creators

Influencers are Troomi's brand ambassadors, creating authentic social media content. The program is structured in tiers to reward performance and offers unique incentives to foster a strong community.

  • VIP Tier: Highest commission at $50 + 10% of the sale.
  • Tier 1: Earns a $30 + 10% commission per sale.
  • Tier 2: Earns a $25 + 10% commission per sale.
  • Monthly Service Waived: A key perk for influencers who have a kid's phone and post at least once per month.
  • Special Campaigns: Opportunities to earn extra cash by participating in targeted promotions.

Partners: The Trusted Advisors

The Partner Program collaborates with businesses and professionals who share Troomi's values. These partners, like therapists or coaches, recommend Troomi to their own established audiences, leveraging trust and shared interests.

Audience: Partners have a direct line to audiences that are naturally interested in Troomi's mission.

Promotion: Promotion is done via a unique code shared with their customers or clients, not through public social media posts.

Compensation: Partners earn a commission on sales. The specific commission rate varies per partner.
